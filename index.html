About

I am Postdoctoral Researcher at Department of Methodology at the London School of Economics and Political Science. I earned my Ph.D in political science at Rice University in 2012 and moved to United Kingdom. I used to be at Nuffield Centre for Experimental Social Sciences as a postdoc officer.

My research and teaching interests lie in the fields of comparative politics (political institutions, parties and elections, comparative political behavior, and Japanese Politics) and resaerch methodology (experimental methods, Bayesian statistics, and computer content analysis).
